HOMESICK

Redesign Dutch e-commerce which targets expats in the United States of America

Clickable Prototype »

CLIENT

The client is The Dutch Webshop 1, a premiere source for Dutch products in the United States. Award winning in advertising Dutch culinary products and contributing to a stronger bond in the Dutch expat community.

1 Name has been altered for privacy agreements

CHALLENGE

The Dutch Webshop had an outdated website that needed updates to match recent trends and standards of modern web design in order to look and feel more in tune with the present to better engage more customers. The target audience was Dutch people living in United States as well as US citizens seeking for international culinary experiences.

MY ROLE

This interaction project was in collaboration with another UX designer. I worked on personas, expert review, requirement list, flowchart, lo-fi wireframes, style tile, interaction patterns, design motivations, and hi-fi prototype.

The goal was to create a digital platform that fulfils the needs of the client regarding having a website with an up-to-date look and feel, adapting to recent trends and user’s expectations.
