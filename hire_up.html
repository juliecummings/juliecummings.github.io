CLIENT

HireUp is a start-up recruitment agency. StartUpYourLife was the origins and is a foundation. After, HireUp along with CoachEmUp grew from it. HireUp handles the job recruitment and CoachEmUp provides coaching for those in need. HireUp is dedicated to an empathetic approach. Due to its ties with the foundation (social work), they are determined to show that they truly care.

CHALLENGE

The usability and engagement of HireUp’s current website is not optimal. As a result, it struggles with generating more traffic with existing and first time visitors. Thus, the challenge is to create an easy-to-use updated website that caters to both its intended users, employers and employees. Bonus if it has extra functions as users deem needed and a striking visual look and feel.

MY ROLE

This was an individual graduation project. However, I was part of a whole with recruitment and social work employees as well as a group of interns in marketing over the four months. I worked collaboratively with different teams and conducted all the different phases of the design thinking process which includes research, defining, ideating, prototyping, and testing.

SUMMARY

This was my graduation project which I worked on within HireUp. The topic was how to enhance the UX of HireUp’s recruitment process experience. More specifically, it was about creating a more desirable recruitment process for the users which could lead to increasing the conversions. My aim was to improve the UX across all stages of the recruiting process as a means to provide a rich user experience for both first time visitors and customers. Another goal that came up in the process of doing so was to improve the company’s branding in order to better showcase its personality.

The main goal was to enhance the UX of the recruitment process in order to have an optimised experience based on customer behaviour and needs. As such, customer satisfaction and conversion rate would increase.

THE PROCESS

I combined design thinking with the agile way of working. Therefore, the following stages were often repeated or improved iteratively.

RESEARCH

To kickstart this project, I raised research questions to be the focus of the research process. Based on these questions, I selected research methods that would most likely cover all of them.

The main methods that I explored during my research and their outcomes were summarised as follows:

Based on the collected data, I came to the conclusion that the design solution should follow these requirements:
  • ★It must offer security to the users, making them feel in control of their actions rather than feeling uncertain about the process;
  • ★It must be authentic and consistent in its own style;
  • ★It must have an identity that communicates coherently with the intended user’s preferences, identity and values;
  • ★It must be transparent and clear about how things work;
  • ★It must be clear, easy to user, and allow the users to be efficient;
  • ★It must present choices to the users and make them feel in control;
  • ★It must be personal and make the users feel connected.
Furthermore, the design solution needed to adhere to HireUp’s brand values (having a genuine touch, a bold and contemporary style, embracing diversity, approachable connectivity, and a caring perspective) in order to fulfil the users’ need of feeling connected to the product and service.
